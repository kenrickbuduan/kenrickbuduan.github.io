About Me

I'm Kenrick Buduan, a Philosophy graduate at University of the Philippines-Diliman. I was born on October 1998. The languages I spoke include English and Tagalog, as well as miniscule bits of Spanish and German. My main interests include technology in general, data privacy, environmental ethics, and a sprinkling of other philosophical topics. Other interests include computer programming, consumer electronics, and a little bit of world politics. My programming languages include HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. I advocate for data privacy, use of open-source software, and environmental rights (animal rights included). Also, I write about things: see what I write about on my Medium page.
